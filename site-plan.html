NaviNiu Farms – Website Site Plan

A tropical fruit farm in the heart of Hawaii

Site Name

NaviNiu Farms — This name blends Polynesian elements ("Navi" and "Niu", meaning "to navigate" and "coconut" in Samoan/Hawaiian respectively) with the idea of nature and heritage. It represents a tropical farm that reflects cultural pride and fresh island-grown produce.

Optional domain availability (not required): naviniufarms.com

Site Purpose

The site showcases a tropical fruit farm based in Hawaii that offers farm tours, sells produce, and shares information about fruit nutrition and cultural significance. The website will include:

  • A visual overview of the farm's story and mission
  • Dynamic listings of tropical fruits (mango, coconut, pineapple)
  • Details about sustainable farming practices and visitor tours
  • An HTML form for contacting the farm and scheduling tours

Scenarios

  • “When is the best time to visit the farm and try fresh mangos?”
  • “What are the health benefits of pineapples and how do locals traditionally use them?”

Color Scheme

Primary Color: #2e7d32 (Tropical Green)

Accent Color: #f9a825 (Pineapple Yellow)

  • Headings: Primary color
  • Buttons and highlights: Accent color
  • Background: Light tropical cream (#fffdf5)
  • Text: Neutral dark (#333)

Typography

  • Headings: Lora, serif (for an elegant, nature-inspired look)
  • Body text: Open Sans, sans-serif (for clean readability)
  • Navigation & Buttons: Same as body font for consistency
